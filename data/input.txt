Nepal is a country in South Asia. Its capital is Kathmandu, a vibrant city. The population of Nepal is approximately 31 million people. Kathmandu has a metro system in development.

Nepal shares its borders with India to the south and China to the north. Its highest point is Mount Everest, standing at 8,848 meters above sea level. The country is known for its diverse geography, ranging from plains to towering mountains. Kathmandu serves as the economic hub of Nepal.

The official language of Nepal is Nepali, spoken by the majority of its people. Its major river is the Koshi, which flows through the eastern part of the country. Nepal became a republic in 2008, ending a long history of monarchy. Kathmandu hosts the annual festival of Dashain, a significant cultural event.
